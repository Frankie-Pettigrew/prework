New Order Fanpage

Biography

New Order was formed by the members of Joy Division in 1980 after Ian Curtis' death.

Members

  • Bernard Sumner
  • Stephen Morris
  • Gillian Gilbert
  • Peter Hook

Impact

Their record, Blue Monday, was the first 12" single to get #1 on the UK charts. It is still the best selling 12" single of all time

Sleeve Design

Their album covers were designed by Peter Saville, and have been acclaimed and awarded for their modern, abstract design
